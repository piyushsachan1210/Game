Welcome to Tic Tac Toe Game!

lets play!! Start from X's turn!

Time is 5 Minutes.

5 Minute(s): 5x60 Second(s) = 300 Second(s)

Instructions/Details:
1. This is a Tic-Tac-Toe game.
2. Two players "X" and "O" will be playing this game.
3. First Player X will start the game and O's turn will come second.
4. Click on "Game Start" button and Board/boxes will appear to play the game.
5. Click on "Reset/Stop" button to reset or stop the Game.
6. 5 minutes timer will start once game will start.
7. Game will reset after 5 minutes of starting the game.
